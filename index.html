About the Challenge

Welcome to the 20 Days of Learning JavaScript & React Challenge! This challenge is designed for anyone eager to improve their JavaScript and React skills, whether you're a beginner or looking to level up. Over 20 days, you'll gain hands-on experience, connect with fellow developers, and have fun while learning.

Our goal is to create a supportive community where learners can collaborate, share knowledge, and motivate each other. Whether you're building your first project or refining your skills, this challenge is for you.
